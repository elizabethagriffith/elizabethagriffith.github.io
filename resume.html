Projects

"Apiary" Logo Design

I created a package design for an imaginary business named "Apiary" for their bee pollen product. The design was minimal and modern, which was geared towards their target market of young healthy individuals.

Bodoni Type Poster

I created a poster that highlighted the typographic anatomy of the font Bodoni. The final poster size measured twenty by fourty inches and used both cases of the letter "j" to educate the viewer on type anatomy.

Typographic Ruler

I created a 2.5” x 9” ruler that contained the metric system, inch system, pica system, multiple common conversion factors, multiple common ratios, and a list of special typographic characters.

Alphabet Soup Plate

I created a 10x10 inch recipie plate which demonstrated typographic hierarchy. Many variations were created that used different contrast systems. The final plate used color, scale, structure, weight, and orientation to make a visually appealing type plate.
